Best practices for presenting compensation information.
  • Provide transparent information about the offered salary range, bonuses, or incentives helps attract candidates who align with your expectations and eliminates unnecessary negotiations.
  • Consider showcasing the full compensation package, including non-monetary benefits such as healthcare, paid leave, professional development opportunities, or flexible work arrangements. This highlights the overall value of the position beyond just the salary.
  • Providing a salary range rather than an exact figure allows flexibility and keeps the role attractive to a broader pool of candidates. It also gives room for negotiation based on experience and skills.
